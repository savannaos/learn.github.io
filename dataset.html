Dataset

Overview

Acquiring the right dataset is vital to any machine learning task. Here we discuss example datasets, diving specifically into the Youtube 8M dataset.

Objectives

  • Understand how to search for datasets
  • Understand the categorizations of the video data
  • Reflect on the intersection between media and problem solving
